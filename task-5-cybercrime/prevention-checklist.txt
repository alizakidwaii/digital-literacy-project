Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students
General Safety ?
1. Do not click on suspicious links in emails, WhatsApp, or social media messages. 
2. Verify the sender’s identity before responding to any account-related request. 
3. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all online accounts. 
4. Use strong, unique passwords for each account and update them regularly. 
5. Avoid oversharing personal information such as your address, phone number, or college ID online. 
6. Keep your devices, apps, and antivirus software updated to prevent malware attacks.
7. Log out of shared devices after using accounts to prevent unauthorized access.
8. Be cautious with public Wi-Fi; avoid accessing banking or sensitive accounts on unsecured networks.
Financial/UPI Safety ??

9. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank passwords with anyone, even if the request seems urgent.
10. Only make transactions through official payment apps and verify links before entering any banking information.
Reporting Channels ??
* National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
